Frequently Asked Questions

  • 1. How much does a custom website design cost?

    The cost of a custom website design varies based on several factors such as the complexity of the design, the number of pages, functionality, and any additional features like e-commerce capabilities. I work closely with clients to understand their needs and provide a tailored quote based on these factors.

  • 2. How long does it take to design and build a website?

    The time it takes to design and build a website varies based on its complexity and the specific requirements. A simple website might take a few days, while an e-commerce site with custom features could take several weeks. I work closely with my clients to establish timelines and ensure timely delivery.

  • 3. Can you help with SEO to improve our website's visibility?

    Absolutely! I offer Search Engine Optimization techniques to improve your website's visibility on search engines like Google. This includes keyword research, on-page optimization, content strategy, and more. Our goal is to help your website rank higher in search results, driving more organic traffic to your site.

  • 4. Can you help with choosing a hosting plan and registering a domain name?

    Yes, I can assist you in choosing a hosting plan and registering a domain name that suits your brand and is memorable for your audience. A domain name is your website's unique address on the internet.
